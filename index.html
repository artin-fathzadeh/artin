Why this symbol-␗. Why out of all the Unicode symbols did I choose this-␗, I had many reasons but I'll keep it to 3. Firstly the last 4 letters spell out 'lock', symbolizing the encryption I used. Secondly ␗ stands for end of transmission block, it was always interesting to me how computers knew when a message ended, just like how I always wondered how you could encrypt things through the internet without sending the key when I was little (now I know it uses public and private keys). The final reason being that its a widely supported Unicode character, not all Unicode characters are supported everywhere, and while some are, some aren't and since I needed to use the symbol I chose in many places, it needed to be very widely supported.
